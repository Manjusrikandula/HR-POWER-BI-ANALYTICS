## Problem Statement
The objective of this project is to analyze employee data to understand workforce distribution, demographics, and salary patterns.

## Business Questions
- How is the workforce distributed across departments?
- What is the gender distribution by department?
- What is the age distribution of employees?
- How do salary ranges differ across departments?
- Who are the top earners by country?

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel / CSV
